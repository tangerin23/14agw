Poster

Applications for the poster will be acceptable
until 24:00 August 31, 2023.
extended to September 11,2023 until noon.

1. Applicant

Your registration must be completed. On your registration, you will receive a reply from the Secretariat by e-mail. The acceptance number on the e-mail will be required for the poster application. The poster application will be acceptable only by e-mail. E-mail title should be “AdvanceGene Poster”. Please attach 2 files (as mentioned below) and send it to the Secretariat by e-mail.

How to make application form

  • Download and fill in your application form (Excel file)
  • File name：“Your name”Poster application.xls
  • Please send it as attached file to the Secretariat
  • Application Form

How to make abstract

  • The file should be Microsoft Office Word 2007 or later
  • File name：“Your name”Abstract.docx
  • Please send it as attached file to the Secretariat
  • Example for Abstract

One sheet A4
Margin：Top 25mm, Bottom 20mm, Left 20mm, Right 20mm
Please attach “○”in front of the presenter name

FONT；

  • Title：Arial 18pt, bold
  • Author, Co-Author：Arial 14pt bold
  • Affiliation：Arial 12pt
  • Summary：Time New Roman 12pt

Your file will be printed directly on the Abstract. Any change cannot be made after the deadline. You will receive a confirmation mail after your application. If you do not receive your confirmation mail, please do not hesitate to contact the Secretariat.

Notice

Your abstract will be acceptable only by e-mail. So please do not send it by Fax.

Deadline

Thursday, August 31, 2023 Extended to Septenber 11, 2023 until noon.
