Meet the Expert

Would you like to join lunch discussion with foreign lecturers at the science session?
Anyone can apply for it for free of registration fee.
Entry registration is required.
You can only register your pre-entry and can apply for “Meet the Expert” at a later date.

Application Procedures

  1. Complete your entry registration.
  2. After registering to attend, sign up for Meet the Expert.
    Registration will be available at a later date.
  3. The Secretariat will contact you regarding your desired date and lecturer.
