Steps to make your registration

All registrations must be submitted through the online registration system. .

  1. Start registration: Click “For REGISTRATION FORM” above.
  2. Fill in the Registration Form：

    Fill in the registration form completely, and then click “Next”.
    If any error messages appear on the screen, please go back to the registration page and fill in again.

  3. Check the registration form:

    Check all information again, and then click “Next”.

  4. Payment Method:

    Credit card only

  5. Fill in the necessary information:

    Fill the necessary information in the payment form.

    • ● Credit Card : Card number / Card holder’s name / valid date (mm/yy)
    • ● Bank Transfer : Bank transfer name / Scheduled date of bank transfer
  6. Change / Cancellation and Refund

    If you wish to change your registration details (address, etc.) or cancel your registration before 11:59 p.m. on September 15, 2023, please click "Click here for registration form" and enter your login ID and password to cancel your registration from My Page. You will receive a refund minus 10% of the registration fee. No refunds will be made for cancellations made after noon on September 15, 2023.
